Guide

Guide to play top down tank

Control

Move: WASD or Arrow keys
Shoot: Spacebar
Restart: R
Menu: Esc

How to Play

The player starts on the battlefield and must defeat all enemy tanks. Enemy tanks move automatically and fire bullets at the player. The player loses if their tank is destroyed.

Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid enemy bullets.

  • Try to attack enemies from a distance.

  • Use the map layout to protect yourself.

  • Restart or generate a new map to try again.
