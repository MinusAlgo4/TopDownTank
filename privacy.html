Privacy Policy

Privacy for User Data

No personal data is sent to an external server. Any account information entered on the Sign Up or Sign In pages is stored locally in the user’s browser using Local Storage.

The Contact form is also a demonstration feature and does not send data to a live server.

Users should not enter sensitive real-world personal information into this website, as the authentication system is only intended to demonstrate front-end web development concepts.
